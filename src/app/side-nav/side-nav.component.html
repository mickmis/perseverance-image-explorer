Perseverance Image Explorer

Colorize and display images from the Mars 2020 Perseverance rover.

This web application talks to the NASA raw images API to fetch the images, and colorize them depending on its type:

  • RGB combined: combine the red, blue and green components of the image,
  • demosaiced: demosaic / debayer the image.

At the moment, images that are already colored or that cannot be colorized are not displayed.

Has an effect only on RGB combined. The demosaiced images are always full resolution.
